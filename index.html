About Me

Er. Madhab Rijal is a Professional Engineer, an Assistant Professor at Mid-West University in Nepal, and a PhD candidate majoring in Hydraulic Engineering at Chang'an University, China.

His academic and research focus lies in the fields of hydrology and environmental science. With a strong record of publication, he has successfully authored 6 research papers in reputable SCI-indexed journals.

His work has been featured in top-tier publications, including Science of the Total Environment and the Journal of Hydrology, demonstrating his commitment to high-quality research.

Complementing his research, Mr. Rijal has developed significant teaching expertise and is proficient in a suite of analytical and spatial analysis tools, such as ArcGIS and Origin Pro.
